The seven major components of a computer are:
-CPU
-GPU
-Storage
-MotherBoard
-RAM
-IO Device
-Power Supply

Motherboard Functions as the central backbone to connect all other components, responsible for power distribution, used to load and contain the BIOS.

South Bridge connects to the I/O device and is called the I/O Manager
